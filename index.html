About

Let me introduce myself.

Aditya Singh

Biologist • Experimentalist • Computationally curious

Download CV

I am a mechanism-first experimental biologist studying Candida glabrata, host-like stress responses, metabolism, and protease-linked adaptation.

My long-term goal is to move from describing these systems experimentally to modeling them computationally, so biological state transitions can be framed as learnable logic.

I am especially interested in PhD environments where rigorous bench biology and formal modeling inform each other. The transition into computation is not cosmetic for me; it follows directly from the questions my experiments keep surfacing.
